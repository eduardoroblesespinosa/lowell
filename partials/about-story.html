From Seed to Sale

It's all-natural.

1. Seed

We start with high-quality, non-GMO seeds, selected for their superior genetics to ensure a robust and healthy plant.

2. Germination

Our seeds are germinated in a controlled environment with pure water, coaxing them to sprout into new life.

3. Vegetation

During the vegetative stage, our plants soak up the California sun, growing strong stems and lush leaves.

4. Flowering

As the plant matures, it enters the flowering stage, developing the dense, resinous buds we're known for.

5. Harvest

Our plants are harvested by hand at their peak, ensuring maximum potency and flavor in every bud.

6. Curing

The buds are slow-cured to perfection, a crucial step that enhances their aroma, taste, and overall quality.
